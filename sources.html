Préservation de l'Histoire du Bureau des Opérations Aériennes

Beyond simply transmitting the memory of the Resistance, the association dedicated to preserving the history of the Air Operations Bureau (BOA) acts as a guardian of that memory. With 99% of its members being descendants of the BOA Region A cell, we have chosen to continue the work begun by our ancestors, whether they were Free French fighters or members of the French Internal Resistance, Companions of the Liberation, or members of parachute drop teams. We preserve their stories and archives and make it a point of honor to pass on the values ​​of the French Resistance. We conduct historical research on the parachute drops and Resistance actions carried out by the BOA throughout the department and in neighboring departments.
